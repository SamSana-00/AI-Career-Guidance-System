Tips for Job Interview Research
Before Your Interview
  • Research the company's mission, values, and culture
  • Review common interview questions for your role
  • Prepare examples from your experience that highlight key skills
  • Practice your responses out loud
During Your Interview
  • Use the STAR method for behavioral questions
  • Ask thoughtful questions about the role and company
  • Highlight how your skills align with job requirements
  • Maintain positive body language and eye contact

The STAR Method for Behavioral Questions

S

Situation

Describe the context and background

T

Task

Explain your responsibility in that situation

A

Action

Describe the specific actions you took

R

Result

Share the outcomes of your actions

Additional Interview Tips
  • Research the company thoroughly before your interview
  • Prepare a professional outfit the night before
  • Arrive 10-15 minutes early or join virtual meetings 5 minutes early
  • Bring extra copies of your resume to in-person interviews
  • Follow up with a thank-you email within 24 hours after the interview
  • Prepare questions that demonstrate your interest in the company and role
  • Practice good body language and maintain eye contact
